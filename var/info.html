Welcome to invento

Invento is a powerful tool that allows you to easily manage your inventory of items. With invento, you can keep track of all the items you own, including their names, quantities, and prices per piece. Whether you need to add new items to your inventory, delete or modify existing items, or even export or print your entire inventory in a PDF file, invento has you covered. Simply use the form on the homepage to add an item, select the appropriate button in the table to delete or modify an item, or click the "Export as PDF" button to generate a PDF of your inventory. With invento, managing your inventory has never been easier!

Invento Homepage

Adding an Item

To add an item to your invento inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the homepage of invento.
  2. Locate the "Add a new item" form at the right of the page.
  3. In the "Item Name" field, enter the name of the item you want to add. Please note that the name of the item must not be longer than 64 characters.
  4. In the "Quantity" field, enter the number of items you want to add. The maximum quantity of items that can be added at once is 1000000.
  5. In the "Price" field, enter the price of each item. The price must be defined in francs, and the integer and decimal parts of the price should be separated by a comma (e.g. 99.99).
  6. Once you have filled out the form, click the "Add Item" button to add the item to your inventory.

Modifying or Deleting an Item

If you want to modify or delete an item in your inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the homepage of invento.
  2. Locate the item you want to modify or delete.
  3. To modify the item, click the "E" button next to the item. This will open a form where you can edit the name, quantity, and price of the item. Once you have made your changes, click the "Save Changes" button to save the modifications.
  4. To delete the item, click the "X" button next to the item. This will remove the item from your inventory.

Exporting or Printing Your Inventory

If you want to export or print your entire invento inventory, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the homepage of invento.
  2. Click the "Export as PDF" button to generate a PDF of your inventory.
  3. A PDF will be printed.
